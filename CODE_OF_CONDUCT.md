# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sexual identity and orientation, or other protected characteristic.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and open to evidence-based disagreement.
- Give and receive feedback in good faith.
- Focus discussion on ideas and the impact of changes.
- Respect privacy, confidentiality, and project decision authority.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, intimidation, personal attacks, discriminatory language, deliberate disruption, publishing private information without permission, or other conduct that a reasonable person would consider inappropriate is unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report concerns privately to the KGAID Methodology Maintainer, Krzysztof Olejnik, through the repository owner’s GitHub contact channel. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as practical. The Maintainer may request correction, issue a warning, remove contributions or comments, or exclude a participant from project spaces. Retaliation against a reporter is not tolerated.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
